## Do I need a visa to enter Montenegro?

Citizens of the **EU and New Zealand** do not need a visa. You may stay up to **30 days** with an ID card, or up to **90 days** with a passport.

---

## Will my phone work in Montenegro?

Yes, most **EU mobile plans include roaming**. Check with your provider before travelling.

---

## What currency is used and can I pay by card?

Montenegro uses the **Euro (€)**. Most hotels and restaurants accept cards, but it's handy to carry some cash for taxis and small cafés. We suggest bringing around **€50–100**.

---

## Is English widely spoken?

Yes, especially in hotels, restaurants and tourist areas.

---

## Can I drink the tap water?

Yes, the tap water is safe to drink in most areas, including Sutomore and Bar. Bottled water is also widely available.

---

## What's the local time zone?

Montenegro is in **Central European Time (CET)**, the same as Sweden.

# Practical Details

## Do I need a visa to enter Montenegro?

Citizens of the **EU and New Zealand** do not need a visa. An ID card allows up to 30 days; a passport allows up to 90.

## Will my phone work in Montenegro?

Most EU mobile plans include roaming. Check with your provider before traveling.

## What currency is used and can I pay by card??

Montenegro uses the **Euro (€)**. Yes, most places accept credit and debit cards, but it's handy to carry some cash for taxis and smaller cafés.

## Is English widely spoken?

Yes, especially in tourist areas — people are very welcoming and used to visitors.

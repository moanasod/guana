## Am I expected to bring a gift?

No, the gift is you being there and celebrating with us.

---

## When should I RSVP by?

Please RSVP as soon as you can so we can plan food, transport and accommodation. Once you've confirmed, don't forget to book your flights and hotel.

---

## How do I RSVP?

Use the RSVP link we sent or write to Moana or Gustav directly. Please mention any dietary restrictions when you respond.

---

## Are children allowed?

Yes, children are welcome, but we will not have any activities catered specifically towards children.

---

## I've RSVP'd "yes." What should I do next?

1. **Book your flights** to Podgorica Airport (TGD) [recommended] or Tivat Airport (TIV).
2. **Send us your flight details** so we can plan your shuttle (if you arrive on Friday or Saturday).
3. **Book your accommodation**.
4. **Pack for the weekend**.

Read more on our [Travel Information](/travel-info) page.

## "Am I expected to bring a gift?"

No! The gift is you being there and sharing this special weekend with us.

## "When should I RSVP by?"

Please RSVP as soon as you can so we can plan food, transport, and accommodation details. Once you've RSVP'd, don't forget to book your flights and accommodation.

## "How do I RSVP?"

Use the RSVP link on our website or reply to our invitation email. If you have dietary restrictions, please mention them there.

# The Venue

## Where is the wedding taking place?

At **Talici Hill**, a restored stone estate above the village of Vrelo Brca, overlooking the Adriatic Sea.

It's 40 minutes from Podgorica and 1 hour 15 minutes from Tivat.

## What's the venue like?

It's surrounded by olive trees, sea views, and gardens — perfect for a relaxed outdoor celebration. Expect pebbles, grass, and old stone paths, so **comfortable shoes are a must!**

## What are the venue rules?

- No smoking inside the villa (€250 fine)
- No glass or ceramics in the pool (€250 fine)
- Outdoor music ends at midnight; the party continues indoors
- Only registered guests may sleep at the villa

## Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available.

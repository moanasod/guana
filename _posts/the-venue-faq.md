## Where is the wedding taking place?

At **Talići Hill Rustic Villa**, a restored over **300-year-old** stone estate above the village of Vrelo Brca, overlooking the Adriatic Sea. It's **40 minutes** from Podgorica Airport and **1 hour 15 minutes** from Tivat Airport.

---

## What's the venue like?

It's surrounded by olive trees, sea views and gardens, perfect for a relaxed outdoor celebration. Expect a mix of pebbles, grass and old stone paths, so comfortable shoes are a good idea.

---

## Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available on site.
